Project Description

Woardlogs - Blogging Website

The project is designed to provide you with the different kinds of blogs which anyone can read and can also share their valuable knowledge in the form of blogs, which they can post it in the website. The advancement in Information Technology and internet penetration has greatly enhanced communication between the author and reader. This blogging site is being developed for customers so that they can have information through blogs from any location. A customer being registered in the website has the facility to read all the blogs and can also post their own blogs in the website.
